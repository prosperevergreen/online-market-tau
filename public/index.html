This is an assignment for the course Web development 1 - Programming.

This web app has webshop like features. Users can register, view products, add them to cart and order them. After ordering users may view their orders.

Some of the users have Admin credentials and they may view all users information, modify and delete users and view all of the orders in the system. They may also create new products, and modify and delete existing ones.

Products and orders may be viewed individually at the top of the page by clicking a product or an order.
